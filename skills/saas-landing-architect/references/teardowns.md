# Teardowns — the evidence (20 pages)

Per-site notes behind the patterns. Use as a reference bank when you need a real
example of a section, headline, or pricing move. Archetype tag: P/A/G.

> Note: most are Framer marketing templates, so some stats/logos are demo placeholders.
> Patterns are real; specific numbers are illustrative.

## Product-SaaS

**Finns** (P) — financial platform for startups. Hero "Change the way you treat your
finances" + dashboard mockup + "4.9 rating". Logo wall "16,000+ companies". Feature
splits (real exchange rate, workspaces, real-time transactions), bento (Smart Banking,
points, AI Split), 4-up feature grid, founder pull-quote, "For Founders/Enterprises"
segments, testimonial wall (5+), final CTA "Start molding your finance team today",
"Join the 12,000+ businesses". Voice: confident, founder-friendly. Trademark feature
"Smart Save™".

**Remote by Modula** (P) — global hiring/payroll. Announcement bar "$2.3M Seed Round".
Hero "Say hello to smarter hiring" / "A hiring platform that works the way you do." +
candidate-table mockup. "16,000+ SMBs trust Remote". Two 4-item capability blocks,
integrations "Less paperwork, more people work", AI CV-matching block, segment pricing
(Startups/Mid/Enterprise, no hard prices), authority "global payroll leader, 150+
countries". Risk reversal "Try 30 days, It's free". Footer "40,000+". Social-proof
escalation 16k→40k is the textbook example. No FAQ.

**Momentum** (P) — "all your AI tools in one platform". Badge "We released V2 open API".
Hero "Access all your AI tools in a single platform" + dual CTA. 6-card feature grid,
3-item split, 9-quote testimonial wall (persona breadth), 3-tier pricing w/ "Most
popular" + annual "Save 30%", 7-item FAQ, final CTA "Start Building with Momentum".
Voice: aspirational, "Say goodbye to endless searching".

**ReadyLaunch / Taskos** (P) — AI task management. Badge "AI Project Tool". Hero "Launch
your SaaS tool, smarter and faster" / "...converts—without writing a single line of
code." Trust "1.2k+". 4-cell bento → 4 alternating splits (escalating depth), animated
stats band, 5-card capability grid, 3-tier pricing ("Best value" mid) + comparison
table, 6 benefit cards, 6 testimonials, 3 blog, 6 FAQ, "Book a demo" close. Pattern:
bento overview THEN deep splits.

**Worklane** (P) — work OS (tasks+clients+revenue). Badge "New — Smarter workflow
management". Hero with animated rotating last word "Workflows/Operations/Processes" /
"Stop juggling tools..." + "4.8/5.0 — 356 users" + "Free for 14 days. No card required."
Logo marquee, 3 pillars, 3-step how-it-works, 4-feature product overview, AI "Beta"
block, stats, 6 use-case tabs, 3-tier pricing ("Most popular" + yearly "20% Off"), 5
testimonials, 5 FAQ, security footer block. Best risk-reversal repetition (3×). Dark.

**LowrCarbon** (P) — AI carbon accounting. Badge "We just raised seed funding". Hero
"Measure. Reduce. Report. With AI." + "Join over 400 teams". 4 benefit cards ("none of
the headaches"), feature block, 3-step how-it-works, 4 testimonials w/ numbers (22%,
18%), 3-tier pricing ("Most popular" Pro), 7 FAQ, blog, final CTA "No card needed".
Voice: witty — "saving the planet shouldn't feel like doing your taxes." Best wit example.

**Alter** (P) — AI marketing automation. Hero "Automate. Engage. Convert. Powered by AI."
/ "Your journey to AI-powered marketing starts here". Enterprise logo bar, integrations,
insights, 6 feature cards w/ scrolling benefit chips, 3-tier pricing (monthly/yearly
"Save 20%", "Popular" Pro) + "2% donation" cause line, 5 FAQ, 7 testimonials "Trusted by
1,000+", final "Trusted by 10k+". Voice: corporate, buzzword-forward. Dark/gradient.

**Lunera** (P/fintech) — personal/SMB finance app. Hero "Track every dollar, grow every
investment." + floating glass stat cards + "40K+ users". Logo wall, 5 feature cards,
integrations, 3-step how-it-works, 3-tier pricing (Free → $25 "Best Deal" → $75),
4 testimonials w/ pain→relief headlines, 5 FAQ, blog. Tagline "Spend less. Save more.
Stress less." Light/airy.

**Dreelio** (P) — freelance business mgmt. Hero "Run your freelance business like a pro"
/ "...without the chaos. ...we've got your back." "Trusted by 7,000+". Device mockups,
3 feature sections (3-beat headlines "Track income, get paid, stress less"), bento,
big-name testimonials (Meta/Google), 3-tier pricing (Free/$189/Custom, "Save 20%"),
blog, community follower counts, "No credit card required". Light.

**Echo** (P) — sales-intelligence/pipeline. Hero "Echo will organise your lost context"
/ "...sells with context, not guesswork." Feature list (One customer view, Clear next
step, Forecasts you can stand behind, Automatic CRM updates, Relationship signals),
mockups (activity, forecast, ⌘K search), 3 testimonials (Head of Sales/RevOps/VP),
about, 2-tier pricing ($399/mo Pro + Custom Enterprise, "no six-month rollout"), 5 FAQ
by category, footer "Get organized. Move revenue." Dark. Strong "show next step" framing.

**LynAI / Saalyn** (P) — AI agent/workforce. Top bar "Experience Next-Gen Automation —
Try It Free!". Hero "Work Smarter With AI" + "reduce manual work by over 60%" quote.
Reuses eyebrow "WHAT LYNAI CAN DO FOR YOU" 3×, integrations "Connect 250+ tools", stat
band, 4-tier pricing ($29/$59/$99/Custom), extra-features grid, FAQ, "Try Free for 7
Days". Dark + neon green. Home = full funnel w/ pricing; /product = mid-funnel,
capability-first, no pricing, "Book a demo / Create Free Account". Good home-vs-product
funnel example.

**AppDrop** (A) — AI productivity mobile app. Hero "AI that organizes your day for you."
+ "4.8 / 500K+ Downloads" + phone mockups + app-store buttons. Explicit "The Challenge"
agitation block, 3 benefit cards, 6 feature cards ("Built for human thinking. Automated
Intelligently."), privacy/On-Device AI block, integrations, 1 testimonial, Free vs Pro
pricing ("Save 20% yearly"), 5 FAQ, 4-step start, app-store final CTA. Dark. App-store
proof replaces testimonial volume.

## Agency / high-ticket

**Agnos** (G) — creative agency. Eyebrow "Digital Agency". Hero "We design brands that
move people" + "Trusted by 100+ global leaders". Marquee, project gallery, "Other
agencies vs Agnos agency" comparison, bento (CSAT, "15 mins free" call card, animated
SEO/perf meters, "120% more inquiries"), industries, founder video, 3 services, project
carousel, 5-step process, 3-tier per-project pricing ($999/$2499 "Popular"/Custom), 6
testimonials, blog, 6 FAQ, "Book a free call — 15 mins, no pressure". Light/warm.

**Startify** (G) — design/creative agency. Eyebrow "2000+ users trust us" + avatars.
Hero "Built for those who want better." Stats band (120+/95%/$1M+/80%), about/mission,
"What sets us apart", 6 service cards, 4 portfolio cards (duration+tools), 6
testimonials, blog, single productized price ($5500 strike-through $6500/mo "+$1000
dev"), 7 FAQ. Voice: refined, understated craft. Anchoring via crossed-out price.

**Nexilo** (G) — AI consultancy. Floating "Available Now" chip, mega-menu. Hero "Empower
Your Business with Custom AI Solutions" / "Accelerate operations, reduce costs, and
unlock new growth" + "Trusted by 120+ Businesses". Animated stats, 5 feature cards w/
interactive mockups, "Why Choose Us" + logos, 4-step process, integrations (10 apps),
4 testimonials w/ metric chips (+70%, 3.5x), 3-tier pricing ($239/$479 "Popular"/Custom,
"20% off" yearly), 8 FAQ, blog, founder-quote close. Dark + video bg. Longest/most
feature-rich.

**MVN Studio** (G) — product design & engineering studio. Hero "We don't make apps. We
build category winners." (negation→elevation, best example). Principles (Human Focused/
Clarity Driven/Built for Scale/Precision Crafted), approach w/ stats, case-driven block,
10-project portfolio wall, outcome cards w/ perf/SEO/a11y meters, 6 service cards, 5
testimonials "500+ reviews", 6 FAQ. NO pricing (quote-based). Dark/cinematic, multiple
video bgs. Leans on positioning + portfolio (note: placeholder stat zeros — a flaw).

**TINKR** (G, real agency) — done-for-you studio operations. Hero "We run the operations
so you can run the studio" / "Your studio should energise you. Not bury you..." + "3
continents" + 🌼 emoji. Problem "Running a studio starts to feel like managing one" +
"you became the bottleneck" + 3 reality cards (01/02/03). "Usual fix vs TINKR"
comparison, "missing layer", 4 pillars, benefits (20h+/mo, 35%, ROI 45 days) + scarcity
"2 spots available", case study (Juno) + client strip, capability tag cloud, 3
engagement tiers (Focused Build / Audit €1,499 "START HERE", credited to retainer /
Operating Partner retainer 3-mo min), 8 FAQ, "Turn confusion into clarity, today."
Best founder-empathy + problem-agitation example.

**Machina (oma-machina)** (G) — custom AI-automation agency. Badge "Led by AI engineers
from multinational corporations". Hero "Your operations, reinvented by AI." + "Trusted
by top 100 ai companies". 4 industry service cards (compliance bullets), 4-step process
"From chaos to clarity", 3 deep case studies w/ $ outcomes ($680K saved, 87% reduction),
stat band, 3-tier pricing ("Starts at $50K" / "$150K" / Custom), review carousel,
integrations + trust (no rip-and-replace, SOC 2, HIPAA, 99.9% SLA), FAQ grouped (Getting
Started/Technical & Security/Pricing & ROI), "Get Your Free AI Audit". Best ROI/enterprise
proof example. Dark/premium.

## Digital product

**Fragments / Fragment OS** (digital product) — Notion "Second Brain" template, one-time
purchase. Hero "Fragment OS — Your Second Brain" + "4.9/5 from 3,470 customers". Two
testimonial marquees bracketing content, 4 named features (Quick Capture, PARA Method…),
6-item feature grid, 3 benefit cards, 2-tier pay-once pricing ($24 Basic / $99 Advanced,
"Pay once, use forever. No subscription fees", 30-day money-back), 4 FAQ, newsletter
close. Voice: minimal, calm. Authority-borrow via PARA/Tiago Forte. Best pay-once pricing
example.
